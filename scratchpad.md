# Digital Smoke Signals — Meshtastic talk (Mo:Dem)

Audience: mostly non-technical festival-goers. Tone: playful & informal.
Duration: ~60 min. ~40 talk / 10 demo / 10 Q&A.

## Design system (Mo:Dem dark / neon psy)
- BG deep:    #0E0A18   panel: #1A1230  panel2: #241836
- text:       #ECE6F4   muted: #9A8BB2   hairline: rgba(255,255,255,.08)
- GREEN (signal/primary): #9DE84B   glow rgba(157,232,75,.X)
- ORANGE (warm):          #FF8A3D
- CYAN (cool/blue):       #4FD6FF
- MAGENTA (psy accent):   #C96BFF
- Fonts: Chakra Petch (display, angular like MO:DEM logo), Space Grotesk (body),
  Space Mono (technical labels / node IDs / readouts)
- Motifs: concentric signal rings, node dots + links, mono "transmission" labels,
  scanline / glow. Section dividers = big number + neon title on near-black.

## Title voice
Short, plain, conversational topic phrases. A couple of genuine questions allowed
for the "explainer" beats (curious voice). Reading titles alone = the story.
